Sublime Text 2: My User Preferences

Jul 8th, 2012 | Comments

 "color_scheme": "Packages/Theme - Aqua/Color Schemes/Tomorrow Night Aqua.tmTheme",
 "font_size": 11.0,
 "theme": "ProKit.sublime-theme",
 "tab_size": 2,
